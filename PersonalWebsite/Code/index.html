Bio

John Frederick Klaine Jr (DJ/Jinn), a late 20s man from Northern Kentucky. John has spent the better part of the past 5 years self-teaching himself programming in order to get a deeper feel for his gaming. He went to Full Sail University in 2011, and left around 2014. John has been writing since he was a child, and has an affinity for psychological, thriller, and supernatural horror stories. In 2007, John first played a game for the Nintendo DS, called The World Ends With You, by Square Enix. This game opened his eyes and turned him onto the path of Game Developement. Since then, John has been trying to get into the business of Game Dev, joining Game Jams and self teaching himself various programming languages.
